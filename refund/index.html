Refund Policy

Our policy on refunds, cancellations, and chargebacks.

Last updated: June 14, 2026

1. Digital Products

All purchases on GameVault are for digital access codes to download games. Due to the digital nature of our service, all sales are generally considered final once the access code has been delivered and activated.

2. When Refunds Are Granted

We may issue a refund under the following circumstances:

  • Payment error: Duplicate or incorrect payment amount.
  • Technical failure: Access code was not delivered due to a system error, and we are unable to resolve the issue.
  • Service unavailability: Prolonged downtime within the first 24 hours of purchase.

3. When Refunds Are Not Granted

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • Change of mind after the access code has been activated.
  • Violation of Terms of Service resulting in access termination.
  • Issues caused by the user's device, internet connection, or antivirus software.
  • Expired access codes where the full plan duration was provided.
  • Sharing of access codes beyond the allowed device limit.

4. Refund Request Process

To request a refund:

  • Contact us through our Facebook page within 48 hours of purchase.
  • Provide your order reference number and the email used during checkout.
  • Clearly state the reason for your refund request.
  • Include any relevant evidence (screenshots, error messages).

We will review your request and respond within 5 business days.

5. Chargebacks

Filing a chargeback without first contacting us may result in permanent suspension from GameVault. If you believe there has been an error, please reach out so we can resolve it directly.

6. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 7-14 business days. Refunds are issued through the same payment method used for the original purchase. We will notify you via email once the refund has been initiated.

7. Partial Refunds

In certain cases, we may offer partial refunds at our discretion, particularly if only a portion of the service period was affected by technical issues.

8. Contact

For refund inquiries, please contact us through our Facebook page. We aim to resolve all concerns fairly and promptly.
