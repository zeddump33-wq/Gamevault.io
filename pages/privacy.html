Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data.

Last updated: June 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use GameVault, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Data: Email address you provide during checkout or access code verification.
  • Usage Data: Game download counts, pages visited, and feature interactions to improve our service.
  • Device Data: Session identifiers stored locally to manage device limits and access control.
  • Payment Data: GCash reference numbers are collected solely for verification purposes. We do not store full financial details.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Grant and manage access to GameVault's game catalog.
  • Verify payments and prevent fraud.
  • Send your access code via email upon successful payment.
  • Improve platform performance and user experience.
  • Communicate service announcements or policy changes.

3. Data Storage & Security

We use Firebase (Google Cloud) for data storage. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all communications.
  • Local storage hashing for access tokens to prevent tampering.
  • Rate limiting on authentication attempts to prevent abuse.
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure.

4. Third-Party Services

GameVault uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Database, authentication, and hosting.
  • EmailJS: Sending access codes via email.
  • Google Fonts: Web font rendering.

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

GameVault uses browser local storage to maintain your session and access credentials. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. You can clear local storage at any time through your browser settings, though this will log you out.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Upon request, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, subject to legal obligations.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with relevant data protection authorities.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us through our Facebook page.

8. Children's Privacy

GameVault is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of GameVault after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our Facebook page or contact us at the email associated with your order.
